About The Vanishing Set

The Vanishing Set is a contemporary literary espionage novel that follows a group of anti-fascist organizers who become disillusioned with the limitations of protest and mutual aid as orthodox tactics for liberation. After a demonstration against a defense contractor turns deadly, anarchists are framed for the violence, police raids escalate, and far-right groups exploit the chaos to target vulnerable communities.

Ana is a community organizer who recognizes that her movement’s visibility has become a liability. Drawing inspiration from a revolutionary folk figure, she recruits a small circle of activists into a clandestine project aimed at protecting people she will never meet while tracking the institutions and intermediaries driving the unrest. As the work deepens, Ana’s sense of self begins to erode, forcing her to confront what must be sacrificed in the pursuit of collective survival.

About Arden Perth

Arden Perth is the pseudonym of a community organizer and novelist. The Vanishing Set is his debut work of fiction. His work explores the philosophical questions of the 21st century, such as climate, consciousness, technology, information, and the collapse of empire through a progressive, optimistic lens of people power.
